Diversity Scholarships.

At NgColombia we are committed to achieve a diverse representation of our community and to provide an inclusive environment where everyone feels safe and welcome, a place where they feel like they belong.

Anyone from an underrepresented group in technology is invited to apply. This includes, but is not limited to: people of color, people with disabilities, people who identify as LGBTQIA+, women and non-binary people, those facing economic or social hardships, and veterans.

The Diversity Scholarship will provide free tickets for the conference and constant accompaniment to those who need it the most. The only thing they will worry about is the positive impact the conference will make in their careers. This is our selection criteria:

✔️ Impact: The impact NgColombia will make in your professional career.

✔️ Interest: The interest you have of participating of NgColombia.

✔️ Sharing: Your desire of sharing the knowledge acquired with others.
